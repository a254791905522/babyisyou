About the Game

Baba Is You is a unique puzzle game where the rules themselves are puzzle pieces you can interact with. By pushing word blocks like "BABA", "IS", and "YOU", you literally change the rules of the game in real time.

Each level presents a grid-based puzzle with rule blocks that define how everything works. ROCK IS PUSH means rocks can be moved. FLAG IS WIN means touching the flag completes the level. But what if you rearrange the words? What if BABA IS WIN? Or WALL IS YOU?

The game challenges you to think creatively about the relationship between rules, objects, and goals. There's always more than one way to solve a puzzle — sometimes the most unexpected approach is the right one.
